# Model 1 - Popularity Recommender

## Dataset

Train Interactions: 61,419

Test Interactions: 7,391

Eligible Products: 4,743

---

## Recommendation Strategy

Global Popularity

Top 10 most purchased products from training period are recommended to all customers.

---

## Metrics

Precision@10: 0.0185

Coverage: 0.0021

---

## Observations

- Strong baseline model.
- No personalization.
- Very low catalog coverage.
- Useful benchmark for future recommendation models.
